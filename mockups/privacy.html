Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Murmur ("we", "our", or "the app") is a voice-to-structured-data iOS application. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Voice recordings: When you use the microphone, audio is captured and transcribed on-device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework. We do not store raw audio recordings.
  • Transcribed text and entries: The text produced from your voice input is stored locally on your device and used to generate structured entries via a third-party language model (PPQ.ai / Anthropic Claude).
  • Usage data: We collect anonymous token usage counts to support the credit system. No personally identifiable information is attached to this data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

Third-Party Services

Murmur sends transcribed text to PPQ.ai to process language model requests. This text may be processed by Anthropic's Claude models. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle API data.

Data Storage

All entries and app data are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Permissions

  • Microphone: Required to capture voice input. Only active when you initiate a recording.
  • Speech Recognition: Used to transcribe your voice locally via Apple's framework.
  • Notifications: Optional. Used only to deliver reminders you set within the app.

Children's Privacy

Murmur is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
