Quick Reference

What else can it do?

The REPL is a full CRUD interface for your entries.

record [secs]
r, rec
Record voice, extract entries, confirm or refine
text
t
Type text instead of speaking, same extraction pipeline
list [filter]
ls, l
List entries. Filter by category or status
show #
s
Full detail view for one entry
status # <s>
st
Mark active, completed, archived, or snoozed
delete #
del, rm
Delete an entry (with confirmation)
stats
 
Counts by category, status, and source
e / e #
at confirm
Re-record to refine, or edit a single entry field-by-field
